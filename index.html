About me

Your website and/or Content Management System should always make you happy.

During my Computing Master studies, I’ve realized that the difference between an average and an impeccable online presentation lies in the approach to project management. While I am an accomplished full-stack developer, especially in open-source products like PHP (Drupal), Python or JavaScript (and GatsbyJS), my strength lies in liaisons with product owners and IT managers.

As an “old-school webmaster,” I love making things happen smoothly, on time and on budget, because sometimes all the project needs is one developer who can point the way forward, prepare documentation, estimate requirements and actually do the work.

I enjoy listening to product owners’ and project managers’ concerns, and I appreciate the requirements elicitation. My goal is to eliminate stress, so I always deliver all that is required.

Examples of recent projects

  • With the help of a few contractors, I have written an NS Information HUB that has custom Media Wiki language, exports PDFs in the typographical quality of books and is easily managed by editors.

  • In three short weeks, working in a team of just two engineers, we created the architecture, budget and implementation of a Business Intelligence API for the Project Management team at The University of Edinburgh.

  • I’ve migrated thousands of articles and users from Drupal 7 into Drupal 8 in four days, including conversion between content and users, taking into account different web and content structure.
